The Perspective

From the 8-bit foundations of the TRS-80 to the multi-megawatt power architectures of modern AI clusters, I have spent four decades at the intersection of power and computing. My work is rooted in the belief that the "Power Nerd" is the fundamental architect of the digital economy.

The Constant of the Power Nerd

In the current tech narrative, AI hyper-scalers are the protagonists. But the silicon only thinks if the power is clean and constant. Throughout history, the power electronics community has been the unsung hero making "cool functions" possible.

The scale has recently shifted from impressive to absurd. We are moving past 100kW racks into the multi-megawatt frontier. This is a fundamental reimagining of power delivery that echoes the first Westinghouse, Edison, and Tesla AC/DC transition. It’s all about the power—it has been since the first electrical switches were developed.
